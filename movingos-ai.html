Graduation strategy

Niche wins faster

Do not launch ServiceOS first. It is too broad for the first sales motion. Win movers, then add adjacent verticals, then general home services, then graduate the public brand.

  1. Step 1Win movers

    MovingOS AI becomes the default AI office manager for moving operators.

  2. Step 2Add adjacent verticals

    Junk removal, furniture delivery, and appliance delivery share the same operational DNA.

  3. Step 3Add home services

    Cleaning, handyman, and landscaping packs extend the core platform to more local operators.

  4. Step 4Become ServiceOS AI

    The platform graduates once the narrow wedge, workflows, and distribution channels are proven.
